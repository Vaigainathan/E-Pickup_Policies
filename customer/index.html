2) Information We Collect

a) Identifiers & Account Data

Phone number (for OTP/login), Firebase authentication tokens, device identifiers, optional display name, optional email, optional profile photo.

b) Location Data

Foreground: current location for pickup/delivery selection and tracking during active bookings. Background: location during active bookings only, to enable driver matching and customer tracking.

c) Booking & Transaction Data

Pickup/delivery addresses, package details, booking timestamps/status, payment preference (Cash on Delivery), booking history, receipts/invoices.

d) Payment

Payment method selection (COD) and status. We do not store card/bank/UPI details.

e) Device & Network

Device/advertising ID, model, OS version, mobile/WiFi info, IP, app version/build.

f) Communications

In-app chat with drivers (temporary), push notification tokens, call metadata (if calling feature is used), support messages.

g) Images/Media

Profile photos (if uploaded) and package images (if uploaded), stored securely in Firebase Storage.

h) Usage & Diagnostics

App usage patterns, feature interactions, session duration, crash/error logs, performance metrics (if analytics enabled).

3) How We Collect

Direct: Data you provide during signup, bookings, profile updates, and uploads.

Automatic: Location (with permissions), device/network data, app usage, diagnostics.

Third Parties: Firebase Authentication (phone verification), Google Maps (maps/geocoding), PhonePe (if used for payment workflow), Firebase Analytics (if enabled), Socket.IO backend for real-time updates.

4) How We Use Information

Service delivery: Create/manage accounts, process bookings, match with drivers, provide real-time tracking, send booking/delivery updates.

Payments: Support COD selection and status tracking (no card/bank storage).

Communication: Notifications, in-app chat, support responses, important service updates.

Safety & fraud: Identity verification, abuse/fraud detection, platform security.

Improvement: Usage analysis, performance and reliability improvements.

Legal: Compliance with applicable laws and lawful requests.

5) Sharing & Disclosure

Service providers: Firebase (auth, Firestore, Storage, Messaging, Analytics if enabled), Google Maps (maps/geocoding), backend hosting (Railway) including Socket.IO real-time services.

Drivers: Your name/phone, pickup and delivery details, and live location during active bookings to enable delivery.

Legal: Law enforcement or legal requests, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Business transfers: In mergers/acquisitions, data may be transferred to the new entity.

We do not: Sell personal data or share location data for advertising.

6) Data Retention

Account: Kept while active; deleted within 30 days after deletion request.

Bookings: Records retained up to 7 years for accounting/tax; location data for active bookings deleted after 90 days.

Payments: COD preference retained with booking records; no card/bank data stored.

Chat: Temporary during active booking; removed within 30 days after completion.

Analytics: Aggregated up to 2 years; identifiers removed after ~90 days.

7) Security

TLS/SSL in transit; industry-standard protections at rest; restricted access; logging; encrypted Firebase storage for media. You are responsible for securing your device and not sharing OTPs/tokens.

8) Your Choices

Delete: Delete account in-app or email epickupofficial@gmail.com; processed within 30 days unless legally required to retain certain records.

Location: Manage permissions in device settings; core booking/tracking requires location.

Notifications: Manage push notification settings; you may opt out of promotional messages.

Analytics: Contact us to request disabling analytics where feasible.
